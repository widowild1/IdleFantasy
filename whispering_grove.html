Lore Notes

Lore notes are uncovered gradually over multiple expedition sessions. Collect all 5 to unlock the dungeon they reveal.

  1. The trees here whisper in a language older than any known tongue.
  2. Stone foundations visible beneath the roots — the remains of something built long before the forest.
  3. A druid's warning carved in bark: 'The clearing at the grove's heart is not what it was. Stay away.'
  4. Corrupted vines reach toward you as you chop. The grove resists your presence.
  5. A gap in the canopy reveals ruins at the grove's heart. The Ancient Forest Ruins call to be explored.
