Runecrafting

Craft runes from Rune Essence. Higher Runecrafting levels unlock stronger runes. At level 50+ you produce 2 runes per essence; at level 75+ you produce 3.

Rune Level Required Essence / Rune XP / Rune Output Multiplier
Air Rune 1 1 5 ×2 at 50 / ×3 at 75
Water Rune 5 1 6 ×2 at 50 / ×3 at 75
Earth Rune 10 1 7 ×2 at 50 / ×3 at 75
Fire Rune 15 1 8 ×2 at 50 / ×3 at 75
Mind Rune 25 1 10 ×2 at 50 / ×3 at 75
Chaos Rune 35 1 12 ×2 at 50 / ×3 at 75
Death Rune 50 1 15 ×2 at 50 / ×3 at 75
Blood Rune 65 1 20 ×2 at 50 / ×3 at 75

Rune Essence can be mined or purchased from the Shop.
