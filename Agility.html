Agility

Agility reduces session time across all skills. Higher levels mean faster sessions everywhere.

Session Duration by Level

Agility Level Session Duration
1 60 min
10 58 min
20 56 min
30 54 min
40 52 min
50 50 min
60 48 min
70 46 min
80 44 min
90 42 min
99 40 min

Duration scales linearly from 60 minutes at level 1 to 40 minutes at level 99.

Courses

There are 15 courses. Success rate scales with how far your level exceeds the requirement (capped at 95%). Each minute you attempt 2 laps.
