Lore Notes

Lore notes are uncovered gradually over multiple expedition sessions. Collect all 5 to unlock the dungeon they reveal.

  1. A crumpled ledger page: names, dates, and numbers. Someone has been tracking payments to a person called the Warden.
  2. Scratched into the wall: 'For those who prove themselves — the Sanctum awaits. Ask no questions.'
  3. A torn letter warning a member to stay away from the eastern tunnels. No reason is given.
  4. A wax-sealed map fragment. The seal is unfamiliar — a shadow behind a locked door.
  5. The last passage opens into a vaulted chamber. At the far end, a reinforced door bears the same seal. The Rogue Sanctum lies beyond.
