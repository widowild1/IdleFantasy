Pets

Pets are rare drops from dungeons and bosses. Each pet provides a permanent passive bonus once collected. Duplicates are automatically converted to coins.

Pet Source Bonus Description
🦑 Baby Kraken Kraken raid (1/100 per player) +5% Cooking XP A tiny kraken that improves your cooking abilities.
🐉 Dragon Whelp King Black Dragon raid (1/100 per player) +5% Combat XP A young dragon that boosts your combat prowess.
🌳 Ent Woodcutting (1/1000 per session) +10% Woodcutting XP An ancient tree spirit that aids your woodcutting.
😈 Imp Pet Demon Lord raid (1/100 per player) +5% Prayer XP A mischievous imp that enhances your prayer training.
🗿 Rock Golem Mining (1/1000 per session) +10% Mining XP A living rock formation that enhances your mining prowess.
🌿 Tangleroot Farming harvest (1/1000 per harvest) +10% Farming XP A magical plant creature that enhances your farming.
🐟 Tiny Templar Fishing (1/1000 per session) +10% Fishing XP A small fish that boosts your fishing expertise.

Pets are displayed on your Profile screen.
