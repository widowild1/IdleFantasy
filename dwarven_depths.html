Lore Notes

Lore notes are uncovered gradually over multiple expedition sessions. Collect all 5 to unlock the dungeon they reveal.

  1. Ancient rune-carved walls pulse faintly. The dwarves who built this place are long gone.
  2. A colossal forge stands at the passage end, its fires somehow still burning after centuries.
  3. Iron golems stand dormant in the alcoves. Their eyes flicker as you mine nearby.
  4. The forge runes are a warning — something was bound here, and it grows restless.
  5. The golems rise fully to life. The Ancient Forge awakens — its guardians await those brave enough to enter.
