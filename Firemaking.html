Firemaking

Burn logs from your inventory to gain Firemaking XP. Each log burned produces ashes which can be scattered for bonus Prayer XP.

Log Level Required XP / Log Burned
Log 1 40
Oak Log 15 60
Willow Log 30 90
Maple Log 45 135
Yew Log 60 203
Magic Log 75 303
Redwood Log 90 455
