Copper Caverns

Skill: Mining Level Required: 1 Unlocks: Collapsed Mine

A long-abandoned mine beneath the foothills. Its copper veins were all but exhausted before the miners departed, though iron, coal, and stranger ores were left behind in the deeper shafts.
