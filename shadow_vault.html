Lore Notes

Lore notes are uncovered gradually over multiple expedition sessions. Collect all 5 to unlock the dungeon they reveal.

  1. A dossier on every major merchant in the city. Someone has been compiling this for years.
  2. A vault door, partially ajar. Inside — crates of stolen weapons, armour, and sealed scroll cases.
  3. One scroll case is different from the others. It bears the seal of the royal treasury. It is empty.
  4. A private journal: 'The Guild of Shadows is not just a guild. It is a government. They have always been watching.'
  5. The deepest vault is not locked. It is guarded. What waits inside is the Guild's greatest secret — and its most dangerous protectors.
