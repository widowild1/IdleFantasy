Titles

Titles are permanent, earned once and kept forever — even if the way you earned them later changes or is removed from the game. Equip one from the character edit sheet on the Profile screen (tap the pencil icon next to your name); your name then displays as "Name the Title" everywhere it normally appears. You can also see every title you haven't unlocked yet in the same picker, along with what's needed to earn it.

Skill Mastery

Awarded for completing every quest in a skill's full quest chain. Combat, Agility, and Farming have no quest chain of their own, so they don't have a skill mastery title — see Guild Mastery below for those two instead.

Title Requirement
Master Smith Complete all Smithing quests
Head Chef Complete all Cooking quests
Master Miner Complete all Mining quests
Master Angler Complete all Fishing quests
Master Woodcutter Complete all Woodcutting quests
Master Fletcher Complete all Fletching quests
Master Artisan Complete all Crafting quests
Runemaster Complete all Runecrafting quests
Master Herbalist Complete all Herblore quests
Master Builder Complete all Construction quests
Devout Complete all Prayer quests
Master Thief Complete all Thieving quests
Flamekeeper Complete all Firemaking quests
Slayer Complete all Slayer quests

Guild Mastery

Awarded for reaching the maximum level (10) in a Town guild that has no quest chain of its own.

Title Requirement
Warlord Reach max level (10) in the Warriors Guild
Marksman Reach max level (10) in the Archers Guild
Archmage Reach max level (10) in the Mages Guild
Merchant Prince Reach max level (10) in the Mercantile Guild
Pathfinder Reach max level (10) in the Agility Guild
Master Farmer Reach max level (10) in the Farming Guild

Seasonal

Completing a Seasonal Event awards a title unique to that event — for example, finishing the Sunspire Solstice event earns "the Champion of Sunspire Solstice." A new title is added for every Seasonal Event as it's released, and previously-earned seasonal titles are kept permanently even after the event ends.
