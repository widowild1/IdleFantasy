Shattered Spire

Skill: Agility Level Required: 40 Requires: Shadow Den unlocked first Unlocks: Arcane Ruins

The remains of a wizard's tower reduced to a field of rubble and warped stone. Magical energy still crackles through the debris. Only the most agile can navigate the unstable wreckage without being buried.

Lore Notes

Lore notes are uncovered gradually over multiple expedition sessions. Collect all 5 to unlock the dungeon they reveal.

  1. Charred stone and melted glass for a hundred metres in every direction. Whatever happened here was catastrophic.
  2. A torn page from a research journal: 'The convergence experiment exceeds all projections. We must press on.'
  3. Arcane matrices etched into the surviving wall sections — a containment circle, poorly designed, clearly overstressed.
  4. A passage has been blasted open downward. Constructs still move in the dark below, following old instructions.
  5. The passage descends into intact chambers beneath the rubble. The Arcane Ruins still function, after a fashion.
