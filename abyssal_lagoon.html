Abyssal Lagoon

Skill: Fishing Level Required: 40 Requires: Drowned Temple unlocked first Unlocks: Abyssal Ruins

A still black lagoon with no visible bottom. The fish here glow faintly and dissolve within minutes of being caught. Experienced anglers speak of this place in whispers.

Lore Notes

Lore notes are uncovered gradually over multiple expedition sessions. Collect all 5 to unlock the dungeon they reveal.

  1. The water here has no bottom. Strange lights pulse from impossible depths.
  2. Fish pulled from the deep glow faintly and dissolve within minutes. What fed on them?
  3. A torn map marks this lagoon with a single word: 'DO NOT FISH.' No explanation is given.
  4. Something rises to the surface and sinks again. Massive. Ancient. Curious.
  5. The dark water parts. The Abyssal Ruins loom below — vast, alien, and ancient beyond reckoning.
