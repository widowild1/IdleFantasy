Skill: Woodcutting Level Required: 40 Requires: Ancient Forest Ruins unlocked first Unlocks: Corrupted Sanctum

Lore Notes

Lore notes are uncovered gradually over multiple expedition sessions. Collect all 5 to unlock the dungeon they reveal.

  1. The trees here bleed shadow where cut. The wood writhes and resists the blade.
  2. A shadow beast watches from the canopy. It does not attack — it only observes.
  3. Old sanctum stones are visible through the twisted trunks. Something still lives there.
  4. The corruption spreads as you work. The canopy hungers for something unnamed.
  5. The canopy parts. The Corrupted Sanctum rises from the shadow-wood — its doors open, waiting.
