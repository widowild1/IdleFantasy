Crumbling Watchtower

Skill: Agility Level Required: 1 Unlocks: Shadow Den

A decrepit stone tower perched at the edge of the foothills. The stairways are treacherous, the floors unstable. Agile explorers have found more than crumbling masonry within its walls.

Lore Notes

Lore notes are uncovered gradually over multiple expedition sessions. Collect all 5 to unlock the dungeon they reveal.

  1. Fresh rope marks on the windowsills. Someone uses this tower regularly.
  2. A hidden cache inside a cracked wall: lockpicks, a short blade, and a coded ledger.
  3. The ledger is a tally of stolen goods and split payments. The rogues operate out of somewhere beneath the hill.
  4. A trapdoor in the floor, recently oiled, its hinges silent. Worn boot prints surround it.
  5. The trapdoor opens onto a carved stairway going deep into the earth. The Shadow Den waits below.
